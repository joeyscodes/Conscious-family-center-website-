Our Offerings

Programs Designed
for Your Family

📖

Storytelling & Literacy

Our interactive storytelling sessions bring books to life. Children develop listening skills, expand their vocabulary, and fall in love with stories. Sessions include:

  • Picture book read-alouds with props
  • Puppet storytelling
  • Child-led story creation
  • Early phonics and letter recognition
🎨

Creative Arts & Crafts

Messy, magical, and meaningful — our art sessions encourage self-expression and fine motor development. Activities include:

  • Painting and drawing exploration
  • Clay modeling and sculpting
  • Collage and mixed-media projects
  • Seasonal craft celebrations
🎵

Music & Movement

Rhythm, dance, and song — our music sessions are energetic, joyful, and developmentally rich. Children experience:

  • Singing and vocal play
  • Instrument exploration
  • Dance and creative movement
  • Rhythm and coordination games
🧠

Conscious Parenting Workshops

Transform your parenting approach with evidence-based strategies. Our workshops cover:

  • Mindful discipline techniques
  • Emotional coaching for children
  • Positive communication skills
  • Understanding child development stages
🌿

Nature & Sensory Play

Set in the lush BMT Garden, our outdoor sessions connect children with the natural world through:

  • Nature scavenger hunts
  • Sensory bins and textures
  • Planting and gardening basics
  • Outdoor mindfulness activities
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Bonding Events

Our monthly community events bring families together for shared experiences:

  • Family picnic and game days
  • Seasonal celebrations
  • Parent-child team challenges
  • Community potluck gatherings
