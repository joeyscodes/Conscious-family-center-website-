What We Offer

Programs Designed for Every Family

Our programs are carefully crafted to support families at every stage, from pregnancy through early childhood and beyond.

📚 Storytelling & Music

Interactive story sessions with songs, rhymes, and movement — designed for toddlers aged 1–5. Sessions build vocabulary, listening, and social skills in a joyful group setting.

Morning & Afternoon Sessions Available

🎨 Creative Crafts Corner

Hands-on creative sessions where children explore painting, clay modelling, and collage — developing fine motor skills while expressing their imagination.

Ages 2–8 | All Materials Provided

🤸 Movement & Play

Guided physical activities in our garden space that promote gross motor development, coordination, and the sheer joy of active play — with parent participation encouraged.

Weekend Sessions | Outdoor

🧠 Conscious Parenting Workshop

Evidence-based parent education covering emotional regulation, positive discipline, and building secure attachment — led by experienced facilitators in a supportive environment.

Monthly Cohorts | Limited Spots

💚 Family Bonding Circles

Regular group gatherings where families connect through shared activities, discussions, and celebrations — building a village of support that extends beyond our centre walls.

Bi-Weekly | All Ages Welcome

🌿 Birth & Early Parenthood Prep

Empowering sessions covering birth education, breastfeeding support, and the transition to parenthood — creating a safe space for open discussion and informed decision-making.

For Expectant & New Parents
