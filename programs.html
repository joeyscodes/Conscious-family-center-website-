Our Programs

Thoughtfully designed programs to support every stage of family development

🎮 Stay & Play Sessions

Our signature program where children explore, play and learn in a safe, nurturing environment while parents connect with other families. These sessions combine purposeful play, social development and environmental connection.

Core Activities

Forest School & Outdoor Play

Children explore nature, build gross motor skills, and develop environmental awareness through outdoor adventures and natural play.

Creative Arts & Crafts

Express creativity through painting, drawing, crafting and other artistic activities that develop fine motor skills and self-expression.

Weekly Enrichment Clubs

Specialized interest-based clubs including music, movement, storytelling and skill-building for different age groups.

Waka Wednesday Excursions

Weekly outings to interesting places, museums, parks and cultural sites. "Waka" means journey - we explore our community together.

Perfect for: Children ages 0-10 | Parents seeking community | Families wanting safe play spaces

📚 Homeschool Hub & Community

A dedicated space for homeschooling families to access resources, connect with other educators, and collaborate on learning projects. Our hub provides support, guidance and community for families choosing alternative education paths.

What's Included

📖 Resource Library

Access to curated educational materials, books, learning aids and curriculum recommendations for different grade levels.

👥 Community Learning

Connect with other homeschooling families, share experiences, collaborate on projects and attend group learning sessions.

🎯 Guidance & Support

Expert advice on homeschool planning, curriculum selection, learning assessments and educational decision-making.

🎓 Enrichment Programs

Specialized classes, workshops and field trips designed to supplement home learning and expand knowledge.

Perfect for: Homeschooling families | Parents seeking learning community | Families choosing alternative education

👩‍🏫 Nanny Intensive Training

A comprehensive professional development program for caregivers, nannies and childcare providers. We equip them with knowledge, skills and confidence to provide high-quality, conscious childcare focused on emotional development and safety.

Training Modules

👶 Child Development Fundamentals

Understanding infant, toddler and preschool development stages, milestones and developmental psychology.

💚 Emotional Intelligence & Wellness

Building emotional awareness, managing behaviors, supporting emotional wellness and creating safe emotional spaces.

🔒 Safety & Health Standards

First aid, CPR, health protocols, safety procedures and best practices in childcare environments.

🤝 Communication & Relationships

Effective parent communication, building trust relationships, professional boundaries and positive discipline strategies.

Perfect for: Professional nannies | Childcare providers | Parents' house helpers | Anyone caring for children professionally

🔭 Holiday Camps & STEAM

Exciting, engaging holiday programs combining STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Math) with experiential learning. Our camps feature hands-on activities, outdoor adventures and collaborative projects that make learning fun.

Camp Activities & Programs

Scavenger Hunt Adventures

Treasure hunts through nature and community that develop observation, problem-solving and teamwork skills while exploring.

Messy Play & Sensory Activities

Water play, mud painting, sensory bins and tactile exploration that support sensory development and creative expression.

Science Experiments

Hands-on STEM projects including simple experiments, nature study, building and construction challenges.

Creative Workshops & Projects

Art projects, music activities, drama and collaborative creations where children express themselves and learn new skills.

Perfect for: School holidays | Children ages 3-10 | Families seeking enrichment | Kids who love learning through play

Which Program Fits Your Family?

Every family is unique. Whether you're looking for play-based learning, homeschool support, caregiver training, or enrichment camps, we have something for you.
