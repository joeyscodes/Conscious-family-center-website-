Who We Are

Building a Conscious Family Movement in Abuja

The Conscious Family Centre was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every family deserves a nurturing space to learn, grow, and connect. Located within the tranquil BMT Garden in Wuse 2, Abuja, our centre serves as a haven where parents and children alike can experience the transformative power of conscious, intentional family life.

We founded this centre to address a growing need in our community — a need for spaces that don't just occupy children, but nurture them; programs that don't just instruct parents, but empower them. Our approach blends modern child development research with timeless values of connection, respect, and presence.

From storytelling circles under the garden trees to parent workshops that transform family dynamics, everything we do is designed with one goal: stronger families, brighter futures.

Our Values

What Guides Everything We Do

Presence Over Perfection — We believe that showing up fully for our children matters more than getting everything right.

Connection Before Correction — Building strong emotional bonds is the foundation for guiding children's behaviour.

Community as Village — We foster genuine relationships among families, reviving the African tradition of communal child-raising.

Learning Through Play — We honour children's natural curiosity and creativity as the most powerful engines of development.
