Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for programs?

You can register by filling out the form above, calling us at 0803518784, or visiting us during opening hours. First visit is complimentary for most programs.

What age groups do you serve?

We serve children ages 0-10 years. Each program has specific age recommendations. Contact us to discuss which program suits your child's age and interests.

Do you offer flexible scheduling?

Yes! We offer various program schedules including weekday sessions, weekend programs, and holiday camps. Contact us to find options that work for your family.

Can I visit before registering?

Absolutely! We encourage families to visit and experience the space first. Schedule a visit by calling us or using the contact form above.

What's your cancellation/refund policy?

We have flexible policies to support families. Please discuss specific needs when registering. Contact us for detailed information.

Do you offer sibling discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for multiple children. Contact us for pricing and package options for your family size.
