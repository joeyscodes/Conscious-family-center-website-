About Us

The Conscious Family Centre (CFC) is a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space located in Wuse 2, Abuja. Thoughtfully designed for children from ages 0 to 10, we create a rich environment where learning happens naturally — through exploration, play, creativity, and genuine connection.

We offer stay-and-play sessions, a homeschool hub, community support for parents and caregivers, and professional training for nannies and childcare providers. CFC is more than a centre — it's a community where families are seen, supported, and celebrated.

Nestled inside BMT Garden in Wuse 2, our setting is calm, green, and inviting — a true sanctuary for families in the city of Abuja.

What We Offer

Our Programs

Thoughtfully designed offerings to support children's growth and strengthen every family bond.

🏡

Stay & Play Sessions

Open, guided play sessions where parents or caregivers stay alongside their children in a rich, nature-inspired environment. Perfect for building social skills, curiosity, and independence in a warm and safe setting.

📚

Homeschool Hub & Community

A dedicated space for homeschooling families to connect, co-learn, and share resources. Children enjoy structured enrichment activities while parents gain the community and support they need on their homeschooling journey.

👩‍🍼

Nanny Intensive Training

A comprehensive training programme for nannies and caregivers — equipping them with child-led care principles, safety knowledge, activity facilitation skills, and the confidence to provide exceptional, conscious childcare.

🔬

Holiday Camps & S.T.E.A.M

Exciting holiday programmes packed with science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics — delivered through fun formats like scavenger hunts and messy play that spark creativity and critical thinking in every child.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

We cater for children from ages 0 to 10 years. Our programmes are thoughtfully designed to meet children at different developmental stages, ensuring every child has a rich, age-appropriate experience.

We are open Monday to Saturday, from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. For holiday programmes and special events, hours may vary — follow us on Instagram or Facebook for the latest updates.

Yes, we recommend booking in advance — especially for Stay & Play sessions, Holiday Camps, and Nanny Training — to ensure we can accommodate your family properly. Please reach out via phone or our social media pages to reserve your spot.

The Homeschool Hub is a dedicated space and community for families who choose to educate their children at home. It provides structured enrichment activities, peer interaction for children, and a supportive network for parents to share resources, ideas, and encouragement on their homeschooling journey.

Waka Wednesday is our beloved weekly excursion activity where children explore different places in and around Abuja — parks, nature spots, cultural sites, and more. It stimulates curiosity, social skills, and a love for the world. "Waka" means to walk or travel in Nigerian Pidgin — and that's exactly what we do!

Our full Holiday Camps and S.T.E.A.M programme runs during school holiday periods. However, elements of STEAM thinking — science exploration, creative problem-solving, arts and crafts — are woven into our regular weekly activities throughout the year.
