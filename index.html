Empowering Families to Educate, Connect, and Thrive

At The Conscious Family Centre, we believe that strong families build strong communities. Located in the serene BMT Garden in Wuse 2, Abuja, we provide a nurturing environment where parents gain the tools for intentional, conscious parenting — and where children explore, learn, and grow through guided play and creativity.

Our approach blends modern child development insights with timeless family values. Whether you're a new parent seeking guidance, a family looking for meaningful bonding activities, or a caregiver wanting to understand your child better — our doors are open.

Join a growing community of families who are choosing to parent with purpose, presence, and love.

What We Offer

A Holistic Family Experience

We've designed programs and experiences that support every member of the family.

Conscious Parenting Workshops — Evidence-based sessions that help parents understand child development and build stronger emotional connections.

Creative Play & Storytelling — Immersive sessions for toddlers and young children that spark imagination through music, crafts, and movement.

Family Bonding Events — Curated experiences that bring families together — from outdoor picnics to seasonal celebrations and nature walks.

Parent Support Circles — Safe, judgment-free spaces where parents share experiences, learn from each other, and build lasting friendships.

Who Is The Conscious Family Centre For?

  • Parents and caregivers with children aged 0–10 years
  • Families based in Abuja and surrounding areas
  • Anyone interested in conscious, intentional parenting approaches
  • Families seeking a nurturing community and meaningful child engagement
Our Programs

Nurturing Every Stage of Family Life

Story & Music Time

Interactive storytelling and musical sessions that develop language, listening skills, and creativity in toddlers and young children.

Creative Crafts Corner

Hands-on art and craft activities that encourage self-expression, fine motor development, and joyful parent-child collaboration.

Movement & Play

Guided physical activities that promote healthy development, coordination, and the simple joy of moving together as a family.
